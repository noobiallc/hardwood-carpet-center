How It Works

Our Simple 4-Step Process

From your first call to the final inspection, we handle every detail so you don't have to worry.

01

Project Consultation

We start with a free consultation to understand your space, style preferences, and budget. No pressure — just honest guidance.

02

Site Preparation

We carefully prep your subfloor, remove existing flooring if needed, and ensure conditions are perfect before a single board goes down.

03

Flooring Installation

Our skilled team installs your chosen flooring with precision — clean lines, proper expansion gaps, and expert finishing touches throughout.

04

Final Walkthrough

We do a thorough walkthrough with you, address any questions, and only consider the job done when you're completely satisfied.
