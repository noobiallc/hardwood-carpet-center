About Hardwood & Carpet Center

Atlanta's trusted flooring professionals — licensed, insured, and dedicated to lasting quality.

Our Story

Built on Quality, Driven by Craftsmanship

Hardwood & Carpet Center was founded with one mission: to give Atlanta-area homeowners and businesses access to dependable flooring installation backed by real craftsmanship and honest service.

Over the years, we've built a reputation across Roswell, Atlanta, Lilburn, Norcross, and the surrounding metro area as a team that shows up, communicates clearly, and delivers floors that stand the test of time. From single-room hardwood installations to full commercial buildouts, no project is too small or too large.

We believe great floors start with great relationships. That means listening to what you want, providing honest recommendations, and executing every job with the care and attention to detail that your home or business deserves.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every project we take on is guided by these principles.

Integrity

We give you an honest estimate, stick to it, and tell you the truth — even when it's not what you want to hear. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Your schedule matters, and we treat your project with the urgency and commitment it deserves from day one.

Craftsmanship

Every board, seam, and edge receives our full attention. We take pride in the details that most people don't notice — but that make all the difference.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is the only measure of success we care about. We listen carefully, communicate openly, and don't sign off until you're truly happy.

Transparency

You'll always know what stage your project is in, what's coming next, and what everything costs — before we start, not after we're done.

Quality Results

We use quality materials, proven techniques, and experienced hands to deliver floors that not only look great on day one, but hold up for years to come.
