Payment FAQs

When is payment due?

Payment schedules are outlined in your project estimate. Typically a deposit is collected before work begins, with the remaining balance due upon project completion and your final walkthrough approval.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of security certification available in the payments industry. Your card details never touch our servers.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes — Stripe sends an automatic email receipt immediately after your payment is processed. Please check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
